The Tropical Midday Reset
Doel: 90 sec
Pijler: Protocol · Tropics
01 Hook & Het Probleem 0:00 – 0:15
Regie

Direct to camera. Strakke blik. Geen glimlach. GFX overlay: "Washing 3x a day = Bad for your skin." B-roll van zwetend gezicht en foaming face wash cut in na eerste zin.

Script

"Living in the tropics means showering two or three times a day. It is not a luxury — it is just how we handle the heat. But if you wash your face with soap every single time you step into the shower, you are ruining your skin.

By 2 PM, your face is sweating, oily, and sticky. Your first thought is to scrub it all off.

Don't do that.

Here is a smarter way to reset your face for the afternoon."

B-Roll

Zwetend gezicht close-up. Foaming face wash product shot. Beide kort — 3-4 seconden elk.

02 Waarom Het Misgaat 0:15 – 0:40
Regie

Terug to camera. GFX: "Keep your morning products locked in." B-roll van droge, gebarsten huidtextuur cut in bij "skin barrier breaks".

Script

"The products you put on at 7 AM — your Vitamin C and Azelaic Acid — are already deep inside your skin. They are doing their job. They are safe.

But if you wash with a harsh face wash right now, you strip away your skin's natural oils. The moment you step back into the air conditioning, your skin dries out completely.

Your skin barrier breaks."

B-Roll

Droge, gebarsten oppervlakte of grafische weergave van huid die vocht verliest. 4-5 seconden. Cut in bij "skin dries out completely".

03 Stap 1 — Alleen Water 0:40 – 0:55
Regie

Pak notitieboek op. GFX: "The QAL Midday Reset" + "Step 1: Just Water". B-roll van gezicht afspoelen met water. Geen scrubbing beweging — rustig, controleerd.

Script

"This is the QAL Midday Reset. Three steps. No scrubbing. Just results.

Step one: Just water.

Use tap water. It cools down your skin and leaves your natural oils alone. Splash it on to wash away the sweat. If your face is very oily, use one quick pump of CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser. Do not scrub. One quick pass."

B-Roll

Gezicht afspoelen met water. Één pomp CeraVe Hydrating Cleanser — close-up van de pomp. Zachte applicatie — geen scrubbing.

04 Stap 2 — Fast Moisture 0:55 – 1:10
Regie

GFX: "Step 2: Fast Moisture". B-roll van deppen met tissue — nadrukkelijk geen wrijven. Direct daarna HA Gel applicatie terwijl huid nog licht vochtig is.

Script

"Step two: Fast moisture.

Do not use your damp body towel on your face. In our humidity, that towel is full of bacteria. Use a clean face towel or a paper tissue. Pat gently — leave it a bit damp.

Apply the CeraVe Hyaluronic Acid Gel right away. This gel absorbs faster than a standard serum and pulls water directly into your skin without making it greasy."

B-Roll

Tissue deppen op gezicht — niet wrijven. CeraVe HA Gel applicatie op licht vochtige huid. Close-up van patting beweging.

05 Stap 3 — The Dry Shield 1:10 – 1:30
Regie

GFX: "Step 3: The Dry Shield" + "Targeted Protection". B-roll van wachten — klok of timer zichtbaar. Dan CeraVe AM applicatie. Dan LRP UVMune Tinted alleen op wangen deppen.

Script

"Step three: The dry shield.

This is where most people fail. Wait one to two minutes until your face is completely dry. If you put sunscreen on wet skin, it will peel and flake off.

Once dry — one even layer of CeraVe AM SPF 50.

If you have dark spots, dab a little of the La Roche-Posay UVMune 400 Tinted exactly on those areas. This protects your spots against the afternoon light — and keeps the rest of your face light and matte."

B-Roll

Timer of klok — 1-2 minuten wachten. CeraVe AM SPF 50 applicatie — egale laag. LRP UVMune 400 Tinted alleen op wangen/donkere plekken deppen. Eindresultaat: mat, verzorgd gezicht.

Regie

Script
